Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

SportPost is a desktop application that uploads sports videos from your local hard drive to your TikTok account. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Access

SportPost only accesses the video files you manually select from your hard drive. No files are scanned, collected, or stored by us.

TikTok Account Access

SportPost uses TikTok's official API to post videos on your behalf. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to upload content to your account. We do not store your TikTok credentials or personal account data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer any of your data to third parties.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the repository or support page of this application.
